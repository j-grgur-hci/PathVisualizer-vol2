Motivation behind creating an app in React that visualizes 4 pathfinding algorithms, was the Algorithms class itself. Many underestimate the usage of algorithms, maybe because of the lack of knowledge or maybe because they have difficulty understanding them. Many of us are visual learners, and that's where this app comes in handy. It shows 4 pathfinding algorithms : BFS, DFS, Dijkstra and A*, but not only does it show their implementation on a grid, it also offers interesting informations about them.
